## I am a fragment of the world spirit, the genius loci of revachol. My heart is in the wind corridor. The bottom of my air is red. I have a hundred thousand luminous arms. Come morning, i carry industrial dust and let it settle on tree leaves. i shake the dust from those leaves and onto your coat. I’ve seen you, i’ve seen you with her -- and i’ve seen you without her. I’ve seen you on the crescent of the hill.
